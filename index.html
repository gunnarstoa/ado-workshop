ADO Delivery Partner Workshop

Anaplan Data Orchestration · Automation with Workflow · Dev → UAT → Prod

Welcome

This is the practitioner lab guide for the ADO Delivery Partner Workshop. It covers the two critical topics that come after the Anaplan ADO Academy course: how to automate ADO jobs using Workflow, and how to promote your ADO setup from Development through UAT to Production.

Use the sidebar to navigate between pages. All lab exercises reference the Meridian Corp fictional company profile. Prerequisites: you must have completed the Anaplan ADO Academy course and have the HR model and FPA model from that course available in your workspace.
